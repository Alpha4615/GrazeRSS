Marketing Description of App
============================

Stay in sync with what's happening around you.

NewsRob seamlessly syncs with Google Reader and helps you to stay in touch with your latest news.

Add rss/atom feeds of whatever you are interested in to your Google Reader account, e.g. latest updates of your friends on Facebook and Twitter, finance news or new images of your family on Flickr.

NewsRob can download your articles in the background so that when you pick up your phone you can start reading, not downloading. All articles can be made available offline, so that you are independent of network speed or coverage.
NewsRob can also download the full pages of articles that are just provided as partial feeds.

There is a pro version available with more features:
+ Night Theme
+ Share With Comment
+ ...
